# Treasury and taxes

The treasury block holds town gold. Residents pay a morning tithe based on their current need fulfillment. The town will generate taxes as long as one town member is on the server, even in unloaded chunks. It will not generate while no town members are online. A breakdown of taxes can be viewed in the second tab. There are several additional ways to increase how much you make from taxes, such as feasts, the founder statue, and town policies.
