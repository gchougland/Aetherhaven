# Geodes

Geodes are chunky rocks found rarely when mining ores and crystals. The town's blacksmith can crack them open for you at a price. Ores, crystal shards, and rarely gems can be obtained by cracking open geodes. If the blacksmith likes you enough he will share a recipe for the Geode Anvil that will open geodes for free.
