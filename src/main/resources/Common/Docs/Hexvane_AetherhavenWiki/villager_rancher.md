**Thalen Meadowrun** is usually found at **the barn** when they are on shift.

### Quests

#### Thalen's barn
Raise a barn so Thalen Meadowrun can tend stock, gather hides and wool, and keep the pantry in meat and eggs.

1. Take the barn plot token from Thalen.
2. Stake and build the barn prefab.
3. Find Thalen at the farming bench and close the quest in dialogue.

#### Thalen's home
Thalen wants a house for boots and sleep, not just straw and feed buckets by the pens.

1. Take the house plot token from Thalen.
2. Stake and build the house prefab.
3. Assign Thalen as resident on the house management block.
4. Find Thalen and close the quest in dialogue.

### Friendship rewards

No milestone gifts are scripted yet. Keep chatting and helping out in town.
