**Elder Lyren** is usually found at **the town hall** when they are on shift.

### Quests

#### Raise the inn
Lyren wants a proper inn so wanderers have a roof that does not feel like charity.

1. Stake the inn plot with your placement staff.
2. Gather materials and finish construction at the plot sign.

#### Raise the town hall
Lyren wants a hall that belongs to everyone: records, plans, and the treasury under one roof.

1. Take the town hall plot token from Lyren.
2. Stake and build the town hall prefab.
3. Tell Lyren in person when construction is complete.

#### Lyren's home
Lyren wants a house like anyone else's: four walls, a shelf with his name, and no speeches about privilege.

1. Take the house plot token from Lyren.
2. Stake and build the house prefab.
3. Assign Lyren as resident on the house management block.
4. Find Lyren and close the quest in dialogue.

### Friendship rewards

Visit them after your friendship reaches each level to pick up the reward through dialogue.

- **Friendship 50:** teaches you to craft **Charter Amendments Table**
- **Friendship 100:** gives you **1** × **Founder Monument**
