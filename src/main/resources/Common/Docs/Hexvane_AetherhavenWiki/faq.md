# Frequently asked questions

Answers here are meant as a quick reference while you play. If something still feels unclear, check **Mechanics** or **Villagers** for deeper detail.

## Will the mod have *this* feature?

The answer is likely yes, but check the roadmap. Suggestions are always appreciated in the mod's discord.

## Where do I report bugs or suggest features?

Bugs and feature requests can be submitted on the mods discord channel. Including your game version and whether you are single-player or on a server saves a lot of back-and-forth.

## Will saves break when the mod updates?

We try to keep data migrations safe, but **back up your world** before major updates—especially on long-lived servers.

## Can I design my own buildings instead of using the existing prefabs?

Integrated building tools are a planned feature for the mod. There's a good amount of configuration that needs to go into adding buildings at the moment. You can destroy/replace a building after its constructed but note that breaking/moving certain furniture/benches may prevent it from being used by villagers to fill their needs. For example moving their bed will result in them not being able to find it. There is a build submissions channel on the discord if you'd like your build to be featured in the main mod with credit.
