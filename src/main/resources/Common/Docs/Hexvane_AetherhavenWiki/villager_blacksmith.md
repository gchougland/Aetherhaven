**Garren Vale** is usually found at **the blacksmith shop** when they are on shift.

### Quests

#### Garren's forge
Garren wants a real forge and a weapon bench he can claim before he loses his mind on Corin's floorboards.

1. Take the blacksmith shop plot token from Garren.
2. Stake and build the blacksmith shop prefab.
3. Find Garren at the weapon bench and close the quest in dialogue.

#### Garren's home
Garren has smoke and sparks at work. He wants a roof at night that does not smell like someone else's supper.

1. Take the house plot token from Garren.
2. Stake and build the house prefab.
3. Assign Garren as resident on the house management block.
4. Find Garren and close the quest in dialogue.

### Friendship rewards

Visit them after your friendship reaches each level to pick up the reward through dialogue.

- **Friendship 25:** gives you **32** × **Rock Stone**
- **Friendship 50:** teaches you to craft **Geode Anvil**
