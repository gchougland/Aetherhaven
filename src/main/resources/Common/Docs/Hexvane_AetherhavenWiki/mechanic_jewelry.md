# Jewelry

Jewelry can be occassionally found in loot chests around the world. Newly found jewelry is unidentified. Bring it to the Vex the Merchant to identify the effects of the jewelry. Jewelry comes in 5 rarities (Common, Uncommon, Rare, Mythic, and Legendary). Gold is stronger than silver and Necklaces are stronger than rings. The type of gemstones determines which stats the jewelry affects. If the merchant likes you enough he will share the recipes for the Appraisal Bench and the Jewlery Crafting Bench. The Appraisal Bench will appraise jewelry for free. The Jewelry Crafting Bench allows the player to craft their own jewelry using gold/silver ingots, gemstones, and life essence. The more life essence provided, the higher the quality.

**Tips**

* Extra jewelry makes great gifts for villagers!
