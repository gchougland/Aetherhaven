**Vex Sunderlane** is usually found at **the market stall** when they are on shift.

### Quests

#### Vex's home
Vex can stack inventory anywhere. They still want a dry corner that answers to their knock.

1. Take the house plot token from Vex.
2. Stake and build the house prefab.
3. Assign Vex as resident on the house management block.
4. Find Vex and close the quest in dialogue.

#### Vex's market stall
Give Vex a counter and a roof so salvage has somewhere honest to land.

1. Take the market stall plot token from Vex.
2. Stake and build the market stall prefab.
3. Find Vex at the stall and close the quest in dialogue.

### Friendship rewards

Visit them after your friendship reaches each level to pick up the reward through dialogue.

- **Friendship 50:** teaches you to craft **Appraisal Bench**
- **Friendship 100:** teaches you to craft **Jewelry Crafting Bench**
