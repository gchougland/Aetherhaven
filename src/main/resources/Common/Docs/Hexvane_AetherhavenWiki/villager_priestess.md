**Serah Thornwell** is usually found at **the Gaia altar** when they are on shift.

### Quests

#### Serah's altar
Give Serah ground for Gaia: paths, stools, and a statue where she can work without kitchen smoke in her hair.

1. Take the Gaia altar plot token from Serah.
2. Stake and build the Gaia altar prefab.
3. Find Serah at the statue and close the quest in dialogue.

#### Serah's home
Serah has public work at the altar. She still wants a quiet room where the only voice is hers.

1. Take the house plot token from Serah.
2. Stake and build the house prefab.
3. Assign Serah as resident on the house management block.
4. Find Serah and close the quest in dialogue.

#### Roads under the dead
Clear restless undead from the roads, then return to Serah for Gaia's Draught and the recipe at your planning desk.

1. Hunt twelve undead on the roads, then report back to Serah.

### Friendship rewards

Visit them after your friendship reaches each level to pick up the reward through dialogue.

- **Friendship 50:** teaches you to craft **Purification Powder**
- **Friendship 75:** gives you **1** × **Shard Of Gaia**
- **Friendship 100:** gives you **1** × **Verdant Catalyst**
