**Gorruk Stonevein** is usually found at **the miner's hut** when they are on shift.

### Quests

#### Gorruk's home
Gorruk wants a proper house off the inn floor, same as anyone else with a plot token.

1. Take the house plot token from Gorruk.
2. Stake and build the house prefab.
3. Assign Gorruk as resident on the house management block.
4. Find Gorruk and close the quest in dialogue.

#### Gorruk's miners hut
Give Gorruk a proper shed and bench so he can haul stone and ore without crowding the inn.

1. Take the miners hut plot token from Gorruk.
2. Stake and build the miners hut prefab.
3. Find Gorruk at the workbench and close the quest in dialogue.

### Friendship rewards

No milestone gifts are scripted yet. Keep chatting and helping out in town.
