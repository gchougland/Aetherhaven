**Seren Fairhollow** is usually found at **the lumber mill** when they are on shift.

### Quests

#### Seren's home
Seren wants a roof that is not borrowed from the mill yard, with a bed that is actually hers.

1. Take the house plot token from Seren.
2. Stake and build the house prefab.
3. Assign Seren as resident on the house management block.
4. Find Seren and close the quest in dialogue.

#### Seren's lumbermill
Raise a mill so Seren can turn rings of timber into stacks for the town instead of pacing the common room.

1. Take the lumbermill plot token from Seren.
2. Stake and build the lumbermill prefab.
3. Find Seren at the desk and close the quest in dialogue.

### Friendship rewards

No milestone gifts are scripted yet. Keep chatting and helping out in town.
