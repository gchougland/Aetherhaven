**Corin Mosscup** is usually found at **the inn** when they are on shift.

### Quests

#### Corin's home
Corin feeds everyone else first. He would like a door that closes on his own mess when the night is done.

1. Take the house plot token from Corin.
2. Stake and build the house prefab.
3. Assign Corin as resident on the house management block.
4. Find Corin and close the quest in dialogue.

### Friendship rewards

Visit them after your friendship reaches each level to pick up the reward through dialogue.

- **Friendship 50:** teaches you to craft **Banquet Table**
- **Friendship 75:** visit them for their next friendship scene
- **Friendship 100:** visit them for their next friendship scene
